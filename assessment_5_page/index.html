Command & Conquer generals zero hour

Command & conquer generals zero hour or C&c generals zero hour is an extension to Command & conquer: generals it was released in 2003. Zero hour has more units to use to help you to defend and attack from the enemy. There is a new mode for C&c generals zero hour named Generals challenge where a player takes one identity of one of nine generals. At the last round of Generals challenge you will face general leang one of the strongest general having different units in different factions.
